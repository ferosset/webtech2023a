Welcome

Welcome to Felipe on Cars! Your one-stop-shop to all things car related. Come along for the ride as I tell you more about my experiences driving the latest (or not) and greates machinery the automotive has to offer. Want to see what's new in the market? Check out the news page where I will give my personal takes on the recent launches or the current talk in the world of cars. About Me

This website started out as a WebTech assignment, but turned out as a passion project. Ever since a you age, I have always been involved with the automotive world, that being cars or bikes. Aged 3 I started riding motorcycles, and by 5 I was behind the wheel of a car and rowing gears. With this, racing became the next step, and slowly I migrated to driving a touch faster round circles on a track.

The passion for road cars is a consequence of all this, and over this time behind the wheel, I have had the fortunate opportunity to drive some of the most amazing road cars out there. This hobby and passion of mine led me to research an learn all the intricacies of how cars work, differentiating what makes them look, feel and work as the best piece of machinery there is. Join me along this journey to unveil and experience the adrenaline these amazing cars can bring us!
