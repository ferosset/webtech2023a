Objective:

This website will focus on targeting the Automotive/Car community. With this, the webpage will have an entire section dedicated to the leading 10 manufacturers and within, a detailed catalogue of their current offerings. My goal with this website is to educate and inform the enthusiasts about what are the best cars available on the market today, and what to expect with the coming launches. Using CSS, I will add features such as image clicking, colors and many more to make sure the user feels connected and best informed

Target audience:

The website will strictly focus on targeting car enthusiasts, as it will have detailed and specific discourse that those not used to the technical terms of cars may understand.

Content that will be included:

To best navigate my page, I will include a tab bar including the About, Contact and a drop down meny list that sends directly to each page of the specific manufacturer. Also, each car that is being talked about will have a map, and possibly video showcasing more information on the specification of each vehicle. Inside each page, the user will be able to navigate back to the home page by clicking a hyperlinked button - this will make the user experience more fluid and enjoyable.
